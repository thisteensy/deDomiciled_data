Change in Unhoused Population Over Time


Watch how the map changes through the years.

Some states get better some states get worse.

Hovering over the states reveals the number of unhoused people per 100,000 in the displayed year. You can also click a state to go directly to the chart visualization for that state.

One state is stands out for making a steady climb from the bottom to the top. Can you spot it?

The state is Lousiana, and this is due to an initiative which began in 2011 and cut their unhoused population by 80% by 2018.
