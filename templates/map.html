Change in Unhoused Population Over Time



Watch how the map changes through the years. Some states get better some states get worse. If you hover over the states, you can see the number of unhoused people per 100,000 in each states.

One state is stands out for making a steady climb from the bottom to the top. Can you spot it?

The state is Lousiana, and this is due to an initiative which began in 2011 and cut the unhoused population by 80% by 2018.
