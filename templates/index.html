deDomiciled Data explores the data that affects the number of unhoused people in the United States.

The Map

Watch the unhoused population change over time with our map, created using available Point in Time counts of unhoused people from the US Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) and the geoAlbers function of the d3 JavaScript library.

The Chart

Explore how changes in population, poverty and availability of low income housing affect the unhoused population using our chart created with US Census Bureau and HUD data.

The Future

In the future we hope to explore other factors that contribute to houselessness, like investment in mental health services. It would also be interesting to zoom in on smaller geographic areas

Thank You
